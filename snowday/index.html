Snowday Calculator Discord Bot

The Snowday Calculator discord bot is a fun and interactive Discord bot that predicts the likelihood of a snow day in your area.

To use the Snowday Calculator simply type /snowday followed by your zip code. The bot will then provide a prediction based on current and forecasted weather conditions. The prediction is categorized into different ranges, each with its own unique message.
